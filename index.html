About


Normie Cash - noun The currency of the Normies.

Join


The Foundation for a Normie-free Land is asking for your help today!

Normies have begun printing thousands of Normie Cash in hopes of taking over the world (again).

To stop them we have sent a few Kekistani operatives into Normistan in hopes of disrupting their financial system. The operatives were able to install the Pepe-Virus in the Normie Cash printing press housed in the Federal Reserve of Normistan.

The virus will allow us to access the printing press and create more Normie Cash for a small fee, paid in Ethereum. By creating more (infinite) Normie Cash we will be able to devalue the currency to 0 (approximately). This will save our world from the Normies.

Any excess Ethereum, Bitcoin, or Litecoin left from the Normie Cash exploit will be used to build a wall around Normistan.
